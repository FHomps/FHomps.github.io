About me

Hi! I am glad to meet you and a French engineer. I currently work at Eviden as Firmware Architect, where I design the low-level programming requirements for its Advanced Computing products - including some of the biggest HPC servers in the world!

Lately, my work has particularly focused on operating the transition of BMC systems to open-source standards of platform management, such as the DMTF's Redfish and PMCI suite (MCTP, PLDM, SPDM, NC-SI).

Background

I graduated in 2021 from Ecole Centrale de Lyon, after earning a Master of Science in Engineering from a double degree program with Tokyo's Keio University, in its Space Robotics Group, under the supervision of Dr. Genya Ishigami.

Top French schools put a focus on training general engineers - Jack of all trades, good at working with and managing domain specialists. As a curious person interested in all fields of science and humanities, I embrace this title. Still, I am particularly proficient in computer science-related skills, thanks to both formal education, work experience and free-time tinkering.

Other projects I've worked on include ReViVD, a virtual reality 3D trajectory visualizer and immersive selector, and ADViSE, a neural-network based software project to autonomously estimate the landing viability of Martian terrain from aerial pictures.
